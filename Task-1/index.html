About Me

I possess a robust set of skills in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React (versions 17/18), and ASP.NET Core. My portfolio includes projects like a personal portfolio website, the Gadhinglaj Colleges website, and a BCA Notes Portal, showcasing my ability to deliver functional and visually appealing web solutions.

I am eager to take on a challenging Web Developer role where I can contribute to organizational growth while continuing to enhance my skills and expertise. Fluent in Marathi, English, and Hindi, I am a collaborative team player and an effective communicator, ready to tackle new challenges and deliver high-quality results.
